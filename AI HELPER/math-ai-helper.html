Problem:

3x + 5 = 20

Step 1:

Subtract 5 from both sides of the equation.

3x + 5 - 5 = 20 - 5
3x = 15

Step 2:

Divide both sides by 3 to isolate x.

3x ÷ 3 = 15 ÷ 3
x = 5

Answer:

x = 5
