Learning is not linear, and neither is personalization. So much more than math practice, DreamBox Math combines an engaging curriculum with Intelligent Adaptive Learning™ to provide a dynamic, motivating experience tailored to each student's unique needs. The adaptive math software evaluates students' interactions , and then adjusts and readjusts in real time, so every learner can progress at a pace and on an instructional path that is just right for them.

Expanding Instructional Capacity

DreamBox makes personalized learning effortless. As students engage with lessons tailored to their needs, educators gain real-time insights to target instruction and drive deeper learning. Interactive tools, reports, embedded support, and ongoing professional learning ensure that teachers can meet the needs of their students flexibly—without adding more to their plates.

Realize Positive Impacts

With continuous, embedded assessment, DreamBox provides actionable insights that matter. No waiting for the next test window—educators get daily-updated reports that pinpoint where students are, where they need to be, and how to get them there. With an end-to-end view of student progress, teachers can make real-time instructional decisions, assign targeted lessons, and accelerate achievement—without interrupting learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. Fill in your details in the registration form and click "Create Account". You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise customers, we also offer invoice-based payments with net-30 terms.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your subscription until the end of your current billing period.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Login" button, then select "Forgot Password". Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Do you offer discounts for non-profit organizations?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team at support@example.com with proof of your non-profit status, and we'll provide you with information about our non-profit discount program.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@example.com, through the live chat feature on our website, or by phone at (123) 456-7890 during business hours (Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST).
